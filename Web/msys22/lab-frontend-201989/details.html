La Vieille Ferme Rouge



Beautiful red color, with purple highlights. With a lot of freshness, this rich and spicy wine offers red and black fruits aromas (blackberry, black currant). The palate is beautifully balanced, crunchy, with silky tannins and a very long and aromatic finish. Since 1970, La Vieille Ferme has been producing authentic, honest and tasty wines vintage after vintage. In some of the world’s best restaurants and bars, La Vieille Ferme has been acknowledged as one of the best value for money wines in the world. 17°C for a spontaneous meal and for pleasure.

La Vieille Ferme Blanc


Upon arrival at the winery, the grapes are pressed using a pneumatic press. After settling, the must ferments in temperature controlled tanks. At the end of the alcoholic fermentation, the wine is kept in stainless steel tanks until bottling. Beautiful pale yellow in color, with shiny green undertones. The bouquet offers aromas of white-flesh fruit, citrus fruit and spring flowers, resh with an attractive aromatic intensity. On the palate, it is full of freshness and rich with mineral acidity. An expressive and balanced wine.

Laurus Viognier

The colour is bright, pale straw with a delicate green hue. Confirming the freshness of this vintage, the nose shows white flowers, kiwi and citrus. Enjoy on its own as an apéritif or serve with Grilled Prawns, Sea Scallops Tartare, white mean dishes such as Pork Chops and Veal Scallopini. Also delicious with Asian cooking.
